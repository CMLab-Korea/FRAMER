FRAMER

Frequency-Aligned Self-Distillation with Adaptive Modulation
Leveraging Diffusion Priors for Real-World Image Super-Resolution

Chung-Ang University
Creative Vision and Multimedia Lab (CMLab)

TL;DR

FRAMER unlocks the high-frequency potential of diffusion models for Real-World Super-Resolution without altering inference. We demonstrate superior performance across both DiT (FRAMERD) and U-Net (FRAMERU) backbones.
